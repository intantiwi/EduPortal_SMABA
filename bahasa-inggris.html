Why We Should Keep Our Environment Clean

Class 11 - Reading Exposition Comprehension
Hover / Click Highlighted Words Click Paragraph Roles
Paragraph 1

In today’s modern world, waste pollution has become a critical threat facing our cities. If you look at our clogged rivers and toxic urban air, it is very clear that we must change our behavior. I strongly believe that we should keep our environment clean for our health and for future generations.

Paragraph 2

Firstly, keeping the city environment clean protects our family from dangerous health epidemics. Littered streets and plastic mountains are the perfect breeding ground for mosquitoes, rodents, and severe disease-carrying bacteria. When we leave plastic trash on the road, rainwater collects in them, breeding mosquitoes that spread dengue fever. This proves that neglecting garbage issues directly threatens our survival.

Paragraph 3

Secondly, trash clogging the drainage canals is the primary cause of urban flash floods. When heavy rainfall strikes a city overflowing with unmanaged waste, the blocked sewer pipes cannot process the water stream. Consequently, water spills over to streets, entering citizens’ homes and destroying public infrastructure worth millions of dollars.

Paragraph 4

In conclusion, keeping our environment tidy is not just a secondary recommendation; it is an absolute necessity. If we want to enjoy clean fresh air and prevent dangerous flooding, every member of our community must act responsibly. Therefore, we must immediately adopt better recycling habits and keep garbage where it belongs.
